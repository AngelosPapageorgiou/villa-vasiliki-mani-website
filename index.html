Discover Authentic Mani

Villa Vasiliki is a traditional stone house located in the scenic Mani region, offering an authentic and peaceful stay for travelers seeking relaxation close to nature.

Traditional Architecture

Built with authentic Mani stone construction, featuring thick walls that keep the house cool in summer and warm in winter.

Stunning Sea Views

Two spacious balconies offer breathtaking views of the sea, perfect for morning coffee or evening sunsets.

Natural Serenity

Enjoy the soothing sounds of nature - the breeze through olive trees, cicadas in summer, and birds at dawn.

Modern Comforts, Traditional Charm
